Project X: Character sprite shows equipment


2015-09-25 by dvolk

The player's sprite on the map now shows the clothing and equipment they have in their hands (at the moment only the right hand).

I'm not sure if this should also work for non-player characters. They're different sprites, so this couldn't just be added to them. It also probably doesn't make sense for the player to see exactly what others on different tiles are wearing and holding.
