Avarice Inc.

Avarice Inc. is an open-source turn-based strategy game.

The game mechanics are very similar to a 2010 mobile game named Greed Corp.

Building harvesters to acquire resources lowers the hexes' health. If they reach 0 health they disappear - along with any units or buildings on them!

The player must thus balance resource gathering with making sure that they have space left for their resources to be spent on.
